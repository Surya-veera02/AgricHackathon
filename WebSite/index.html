About the Game

Our VR game takes you on a unique journey, experiencing life through the eyes of a squirrel. Collect acorns, climb trees, and store nuts to survive the winter while learning about the impact of deforestation and climate change.

Educational Resources

Did you know? Forests absorb nearly 2.6 billion tons of carbon dioxide every year, helping combat climate change. By planting trees and protecting natural habitats, we can preserve ecosystems and create a sustainable future.
