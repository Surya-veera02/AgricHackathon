About the Game

Our VR game offers an immersive experience where players navigate the world as a squirrel and a lumberjack. You'll learn about the behaviors of squirrels, including their nut-burying habits, and how these actions contribute to forest health and climate change mitigation. You can also play as a lumberjack, clearing land responsibly while learning about sustainable forestry.

Squirrels & Climate Change

Squirrels play a vital role in forest regeneration and climate mitigation through their seed-burying behaviors, while lumberjacks highlight human interaction with forests. Together, they demonstrate the balance of nature and sustainable practices.
